Instant Pot Coconut Chicken Curry
A from-scratch coconut chicken curry built on an onion-tomato-ginger-garlic masala, finished with full-fat coconut milk and lime. Dairy-free and gluten-free. Source: pipingpotcurry.com (4.91 stars, 585 votes).

Servings: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

INGREDIENTS
• 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
• 1 teaspoon cumin seeds, optional
• 1 cup yellow onion, diced
• 0.5 tablespoon ginger, grated
• 0.5 tablespoon garlic, minced
• 1 cup tomato puree (canned, or 2 blended tomatoes)
• 1 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs, cut into 1-inch pieces
• 0.25 cup water
• 1 cup full-fat unsweetened coconut milk
• 1 teaspoon garam masala
• 1 tablespoon lime juice
• 1 teaspoon ground cumin
• 0.5 teaspoon ground turmeric
• 1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder (or paprika)
• 1 teaspoon coriander powder
• 1 teaspoon salt
• Cilantro, to garnish

STEPS
1. Set Instant Pot to Sauté and heat. Add ghee, then cumin seeds; sizzle 30 seconds.
2. Add onion, ginger, and garlic. Sauté about 3 minutes until onions are golden.
3. Add tomato puree and all ground spices. Sauté 2-3 minutes.
4. Add chicken; stir and sauté 3-4 minutes to build flavor.
5. Add water and stir, scraping the bottom. Cancel Sauté, seal the lid, and set the vent to sealing.
6. Pressure cook on high for 4 minutes, then quick-release.
7. Stir in coconut milk and garam masala; simmer on Sauté 4-5 minutes until thickened.
8. Stir in lime juice and garnish with cilantro. Serve over basmati rice or with naan.

NOTES
Chicken breast works but thighs are best under pressure. Stir in a handful of baby spinach at the end for greens. If your pot scorches easily, add the tomato puree after the chicken and water step and don't stir before sealing.
