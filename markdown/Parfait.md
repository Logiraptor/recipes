Greek Yogurt Parfait with Berries and Granola
A no-cook, grab-and-go breakfast with tons of protein — layer it the night before for a stress-free morning.

Servings: 2

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups plain whole-milk Greek yogurt
• 1 cups mixed fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
• 0.5 cups granola
• 2 tablespoons honey
• 0.5 teaspoons vanilla extract

STEPS
1. Season the yogurt: Stir 2 tablespoons honey and 0.5 teaspoons vanilla extract into 2 cups plain whole-milk Greek yogurt until combined.
2. Layer the parfait: In a bowl or jar, layer half the yogurt, then 1 cups mixed fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries), then 0.5 cups granola, then remaining yogurt. Drizzle with extra honey if desired.
3. Serve or store: Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate overnight (keep granola separate until serving to maintain crunch).

NOTES
Toddler tip: serve as a deconstructed bowl with yogurt, berries, and a small sprinkle of granola separately — great for self-feeding. Add granola just before serving to keep it crunchy. Can be prepped in mason jars the night before (keep granola separate).
