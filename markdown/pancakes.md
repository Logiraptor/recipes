Here's a classic, fluffy **pancake recipe** with easy steps using simple ingredients:

Servings: 8 pancakes

**Ingredients**
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ¼ cups milk
- 1 large egg
- 3 tablespoons melted butter
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract (optional)

**Instructions**
1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk together milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla (if using).
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined; lumps are ok—do not overmix [inspiredtaste](https://www.inspiredtaste.net/24593/essential-pancake-recipe/).
4. Heat a nonstick skillet or griddle over medium to medium-high heat. Lightly grease with butter or oil.
5. Scoop about ¼ cup of batter per pancake onto the skillet. Cook until you see bubbles form and the edges look set, 1-2 minutes, then flip and cook until golden, another 1-2 minutes [inspiredtaste](https://www.inspiredtaste.net/24ential-pancake-recipe/).
6. Serve warm with syrup, butter, or your favorite toppings.

**Tips**
- Do not overmix the batter—this keeps your pancakes fluffy.
- You can add blueberries or chocolate chips by folding them into the batter just before cooking [laurafuentes](https://www.laurafuentes.com/fluffy-pancakes-recipe/).
- Leftovers can be refrigerated up to a week or frozen for 2 months [inspiredtaste](https://www.inspiredtaste.net/24593/essential-pancake-recipe/).
