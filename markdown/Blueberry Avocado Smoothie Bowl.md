Wild Blueberry & Avocado Smoothie Bowl
A vibrant, creamy smoothie bowl packed with omega-3s, antioxidants, and gut-friendly ingredients.

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cups frozen wild blueberries
• 0.5 cups frozen banana, sliced
• 1 cups baby spinach
• 1 tablespoons chia seeds
• 0.5 cups unsweetened coconut milk
• 0.3 avocado
• 3 tablespoons hemp seeds
• 0.5 teaspoons fresh ginger, grated

STEPS
1. Blend the base: Add 1 cups frozen wild blueberries, 0.5 cups frozen banana, sliced, 1 cups baby spinach, 1 tablespoons chia seeds, 0.5 cups unsweetened coconut milk, 0.3 avocado, and 0.5 teaspoons fresh ginger, grated to a high-speed blender. Blend until completely smooth and very thick.
2. Top & serve: Pour into a wide bowl. Top with 3 tablespoons hemp seeds. Serve immediately.

NOTES
Use a tamper if your blender has one — keep it thick so toppings don't sink. Wild blueberries have higher antioxidant content than cultivated varieties.
