Blueberry Avocado Smoothie
A quick, drinkable smoothie the whole family can share — toddler-friendly and one-handed.

INGREDIENTS
• 1 cups frozen blueberries
• 0.5 cups frozen banana, sliced
• 1 cups baby spinach
• 1 tablespoons chia seeds
• 1 cups unsweetened coconut milk
• 0.3 avocado

STEPS
1. Blend & pour: Add all ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth, adding a splash more coconut milk if needed. Pour into cups and serve.

NOTES
Skip the bowl — a drinkable smoothie is easier to handle while wrangling kids. Pour some into a straw cup for the toddler.
