Turmeric Salmon Sheet Pan with Roasted Vegetables
Anti-inflammatory powerhouse — turmeric-spiced salmon with colorful roasted vegetables, all on one sheet pan in under 30 minutes.

Servings: 4
Total Time: 25 minutes

INGREDIENTS
• 4 wild salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
• 1 pound broccoli florets
• 1 large sweet potato, cut into 0.5-inch cubes
• 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
• 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
• 1.5 teaspoons ground turmeric
• 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
• 0.5 teaspoon garlic powder
• 0.5 teaspoon smoked paprika
• 0.75 teaspoon salt, divided
• 1 lemon, half juiced and half sliced into rounds
• 2 garlic cloves, minced
• 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
• Fresh parsley or cilantro, for garnish

STEPS
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a large sheet pan with parchment paper.
2. Roast the vegetables: Toss sweet potato, broccoli, and bell pepper with 2 tablespoons olive oil, 0.5 teaspoon turmeric, smoked paprika, and 0.5 teaspoon salt. Spread in a single layer on the sheet pan and roast for 10 minutes.
3. Season the salmon: While vegetables roast, mix remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil with 1 teaspoon turmeric, black pepper, garlic powder, minced garlic, grated ginger, lemon juice, and 0.25 teaspoon salt. Rub the mixture over the salmon fillets.
4. Add salmon to the pan: Remove sheet pan from oven, push vegetables to the edges, and place salmon fillets in the center. Lay lemon slices over the salmon. Return to oven and roast 12-15 minutes until salmon flakes easily with a fork.
5. Serve with fresh parsley or cilantro. Pairs well with quinoa.
