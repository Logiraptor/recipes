Mediterranean Baked Cod with Tomatoes and Olives
Tender cod baked in a garlicky tomato-olive sauce with capers and fresh herbs — Mediterranean flavors with minimal effort.

Servings: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes

INGREDIENTS
• 4 cod fillets (about 6 oz each)
• 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
• 0.5 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and halved
• 2 tablespoons capers, drained
• 4 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
• 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil, divided
• 1 teaspoon dried oregano
• 0.5 teaspoon red pepper flakes
• 0.5 teaspoon salt
• 0.25 teaspoon black pepper
• 1 lemon, half juiced and half cut into wedges
• 0.25 cup fresh parsley, chopped
• 2 tablespoons fresh basil, torn
• Crusty whole grain bread or roasted sweet potato, for serving

STEPS
1. Preheat oven to 400°F.
2. Build the base: In a large oven-safe skillet or baking dish, toss cherry tomatoes, olives, capers, garlic, 2 tablespoons olive oil, oregano, and red pepper flakes. Spread in an even layer.
3. Roast the tomatoes: Bake for 10 minutes until the tomatoes start to soften and blister.
4. Add the fish: Remove the dish from the oven. Nestle the cod fillets into the tomato mixture. Drizzle with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and squeeze lemon juice over the top.
5. Bake the cod: Return to the oven and bake 12-15 minutes until the cod is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.
