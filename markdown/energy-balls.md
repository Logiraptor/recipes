No-Bake Oatmeal Peanut Butter Energy Balls
No oven required — roll them up Sunday and snack all week. Packed with oats, peanut butter, and just enough chocolate to feel like a treat.

INGREDIENTS
• 2 cups rolled oats
• 0.5 cups peanut butter (or almond butter)
• 0.3 cups honey
• 0.5 cups mini chocolate chips
• 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 0.3 cups ground flaxseed (optional, adds fiber)
• 0.5 cups shredded coconut (optional)

STEPS
1. Mix everything together: In a large bowl, mix together 2 cups rolled oats, 0.5 cups peanut butter (or almond butter), 0.3 cups honey, 0.5 cups mini chocolate chips, 1 teaspoons vanilla extract, and 0.3 cups ground flaxseed (optional, adds fiber) and 0.5 cups shredded coconut (optional) if using. Stir until fully combined.
2. Chill the mixture: Cover the bowl and refrigerate for 30 minutes. This makes the mixture firm enough to roll.
3. Roll into balls: Once chilled, roll the mixture into 1-inch balls using your hands. Place on a parchment-lined tray or plefrigerate in an airtight container for up to 1 week. Grab 2–3 per snack serving.

NOTES
Store in an airtight container in the fridge for up to 1 week, or freeze for 3 months. Toddler tip: these are a perfect toddler snack — soft, not too sweet, and easy to hold. Skip the flaxseed if your toddler is picky about texture. Makes about 24 balls.
