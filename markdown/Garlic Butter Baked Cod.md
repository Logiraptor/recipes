Garlic Butter Baked Cod
Tender cod fillets baked under a simple garlic-butter-parsley paste with lemon — fast, mild, and kid-friendly. Works great with frozen cod. Source: budgetbytes.com (4.59 stars, 90 votes).

Servings: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 20 minutes
Total Time: 30 minutes

INGREDIENTS
• 1 pound cod (4 fillets, about 4 oz each)
• 4 tablespoons butter, room temperature
• 2 tablespoons olive oil
• 4 garlic cloves, minced
• 0.25 teaspoon paprika
• 0.25 teaspoon salt
• 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, finely chopped (or 1 teaspoon dried)
• 1 lemon

STEPS
1. Preheat oven to 400°F. If using frozen cod, thaw in a sealed bag under cold water for 10-15 minutes, then pat dry.
2. In a small bowl, combine the butter, olive oil, garlic, paprika, salt, and parsley. Stir into a fairly smooth paste.
3. Place the cod fillets in a baking dish. Spread the garlic-butter mixture evenly over each fillet.
4. Slice half the lemon and lay the slices on top of the fish; cut the other half into wedges for serving.
5. Bake 15-20 minutes, until the fish flakes easily and reaches 145°F internal (thicker fillets or ceramic dishes may take longer).
6. Spoon the pan juices over the fish and serve immediately with lemon wedges.

NOTES
Very mild and toddler-friendly. Serve with rice, roasted potatoes, or crusty bread to soak up the garlic butter, plus a simple green vegetable on the side.
