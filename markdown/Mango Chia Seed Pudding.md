Mango Chia Seed Pudding
Creamy overnight chia pudding with fresh mango and coconut milk — no cooking required.

INGREDIENTS
• 2 large champagne mangoes
• 0.5 cups vanilla yogurt
• 1 cups coconut milk
• 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
• 3 tablespoons chia seeds

STEPS
1. Blend base: Add mango, yogurt, coconut milk, and vanilla extract to a blender. Blend until smooth.
2. Mix chia: Pour into a bowl or jar and stir in 3 tablespoons chia seeds. Mix well.
3. Rest overnight: Cover and refrigerate overnight.
4. Serve: Top with additional mango chunks, nuts of choice, and/or desiccated coconut.

NOTES
Use 4 tablespoons chia seeds for a thicker pudding. Champagne mangoes (Ataulfo) are sweeter and less fibrous than regular mangoes.
