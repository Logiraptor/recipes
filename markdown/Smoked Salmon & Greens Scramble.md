Smoked Salmon & Greens Scramble
A savory, satisfying egg scramble with anti-inflammatory powerhouses — salmon, leafy greens, and healthy fats.

INGREDIENTS
• 3 large eggs
• 60 grams smoked salmon
• 1 cups baby spinach
• 0.3 avocado, diced
• 1 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
• 2 garlic cloves, minced
• 0.3 yellow onion, thinly sliced
• 0.5 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
• 1 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
• 0.3 teaspoons black pepper

STEPS
1. Sauté aromatics: Warm 1 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Add 0.3 yellow onion, thinly sliced and 2 garlic cloves, minced, sauté until softened and fragrant.
2. Wilt the greens: Add 0.5 cups cherry tomatoes, halved and 1 cups baby spinach to the pan. Stir until the greens are just wilted.
3. Scramble the eggs: Whisk 3 large eggs with 0.3 teaspoons black pepper and pour into the pan. Stir gently over low heat until just set but still creamy.
4. Finish & serve: Remove from heat. Fold in 60 grams smoked salmon. Plate and top with 0.3 avocado, diced and 1 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped.

NOTES
Don't overcook the eggs — low and slow keeps them tender. Smoked salmon is added off-heat to preserve its omega-3s. Serve with whole grain toast or rye crispbreads.
