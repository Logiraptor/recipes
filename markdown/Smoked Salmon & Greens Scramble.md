Smoked Salmon & Spinach Scramble
A fast, one-pan egg scramble with salmon and greens — no knife work required.

INGREDIENTS
• 4 large eggs
• 60 grams smoked salmon
• 1 cups baby spinach
• 0.3 avocado, sliced
• 1 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil

STEPS
1. Cook: Warm 1 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Toss in 1 cups baby spinach and stir until wilted. Crack in 4 large eggs and scramble until just set. Tear 60 grams smoked salmon into the pan and stir through.
2. Serve: Plate and top with 0.3 avocado, sliced. Serve with toast.

NOTES
Bumped to 4 eggs so there's enough to share with a toddler. No mincing, no dicing — just tear the salmon by hand.
